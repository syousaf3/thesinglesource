FAQ

Questions we get (mostly from our moms)

Wait, so AI writes the whole thing?
Yes and no. AI reads and synthesizes the stories — that part would take a human team of 5 people and a lot of Red Bull. But the pipeline, prompts, editorial rules, and quality checks are all designed by a human who cares way too much about newsletters. Think of it as AI doing the grunt work so the editorial vision stays sharp.
Wait, it's actually free? What's the catch?
No catch. We're not funded by VCs who'll eventually ruin everything. We're funded by caffeine and the generosity of readers who buy us the occasional coffee. If this saves you 25 minutes every morning and you want to throw us a few bucks, amazing. If not, keep reading — we'll love you anyway. (But we'll love you slightly more with coffee.)
What's "multi-source merging"?
When OpenAI raises $122B, five newsletters will cover it. Each one adds different details — one mentions the valuation, another the investor list, another the product plans. We merge all five into one comprehensive story and show you "[🔗5]" so you know it's important. No other newsletter does this.
How do you personalize for my city?
When you subscribe, you tell us your city. Every morning, we pull your city's top local headlines from Google News and your weather from wttr.in. Your friend in Atlanta gets Atlanta news. You get yours. Same great national brief, different local flavor.
What if I already read one of those newsletters?
Then you'll appreciate us even more. Think of it this way: you can keep reading Morning Brew for the vibes AND read The Single Source to catch the 20 stories Morning Brew didn't mention. Or just read us and reclaim 25 minutes of your morning.
Can I really unsubscribe anytime?
Yes. One click. No guilt trip. No "are you SURE?" popups. No sad puppy GIF. (Okay maybe a small sad puppy GIF. We're only human.)
