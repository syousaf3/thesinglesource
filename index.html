FAQ

Questions we get (mostly from our moms)

Wait, so AI writes the whole thing?
Yes and no. AI reads, extracts, merges, and synthesizes — that part would take a human team and a lot of Red Bull. But the 6-stage pipeline, editorial rules, anti-hallucination checks, and quality standards are all designed by a human who cares way too much about newsletters. Think of it as AI doing the grunt work so the editorial vision stays sharp.
What’s “multi-source merging”?
When SpaceX files a $1.75T IPO, five newsletters cover it. Each adds different details — one mentions the valuation, another the investor list, another the Grok mandate for advisors. We merge all five into one comprehensive story and show you “[🔗5]” so you know it’s important. No other newsletter does this.
What’s the Magic 8-Stock?
Every day, our AI picks 8 stocks that might move based on the day’s news and writes a witty one-liner take for each. Example: “$CVX ▲ UP — Oil prices climbing faster than my stress during tax season.” It’s fun, directional, and absolutely not financial advice. We include a disclaimer because lawyers exist.
How do you personalize for my city?
When you subscribe, you tell us your city. Every morning, we pull your city’s top local headlines and weather. Your friend in Atlanta gets Atlanta news. You get yours. Same great national brief, different local flavor.
What if I already read one of those newsletters?
Then you’ll appreciate us even more. Keep reading Morning Brew for the vibes AND read The Single Source to catch the 20 stories Morning Brew didn’t mention. Or just read us and reclaim 25 minutes of your morning. Either way, you win.
How do you prevent AI hallucinations?
Multi-stage defense: Stage 1-2 extract only from source text. Stage 4 (Chief Editor) is explicitly told to NEVER add information — only fix grammar and flag thin summaries. Stage 5 is a dedicated fact-check layer. If a claim can’t be traced back to the newsletter source text, it doesn’t make the brief.
Can I really unsubscribe anytime?
Yes. One click at the bottom of every email. Instant. No guilt trip. No “are you SURE?” popups. No sad puppy GIF. (Okay maybe a small one. We’re only human.)
