Walk-Forward Validation — "We Didn't Cheat"

What is this and why should you care?

Most trading systems show "backtested" results — meaning they optimized settings on historical data, then tested on the same data. That's like studying the answer key and bragging about your test score.

We use walk-forward validation: optimize on 60 days, then test on the next 30 days the system has never seen. Roll forward, repeat. 8 times.

  • 8 folds of 60-day training → 30-day testing
  • Optimizer never sees the test data
  • Covers bull markets, bear markets, and sideways chop
  • Results include the Feb-Apr 2025 crash (Fold 3 was ugly: -60%. We're showing it anyway.)
💀 Fold 3 (Feb-Apr 2025): The market crashed and NERVE lost money on all 3 positions. Total return: -60.9%. We're showing you this because hiding bad months is what scammy signal services do. NERVE doesn't protect you in bear markets — no stock-picking strategy does. What it DOES is recover and compound when markets normalize.
